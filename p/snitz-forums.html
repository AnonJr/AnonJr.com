Snitz Forums

Back in 2004, my boss asked me to integrate a support forum into our LMS. Since the system was written in ASP Classic, I looked through my options and settled on Snitz Forums.

I've been an active member in the support forums for this project ever since - helping others, contributing various extensions, and working with the core dev team on the 3.4.07 release.

I've not been as active as I'd like these days, but hopefully that will change soon…
